Simon Fisher

Simon Fisher

I am a data scientist focused on conducting analysis to inform data driven decision making. I am particularly interest in applying mathematical and econometric analysis to understand various problems and inefficiencies in the football industry. I have experience working with multiple MLS clubs.

Most recently, I worked with Nashville S.C. as a Technical Performance Data & Recruitment Analyst during their inaugural season that saw them progress to the MLS Conference semi-final. While I was there, I aided key staff members by processing and analyzing league wide tracking/event data in order to present actionable insights that would propel the team forward.

I have been around the top levels of the game as both a player and an analyst. As a player I grew up competing in the US Development Academy system and went on to play college soccer at the division 1 level.
